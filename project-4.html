The Challenge

The logo for Bean & Brew Caffe is designed to be a visual metaphor for comfort and quality. The centerpiece features a stylized coffee cup with fluid, rising steam lines, capturing the dynamic aroma of a freshly brewed cup. The addition of organic coffee beans reinforces the brand's focus on natural, high-quality ingredients.

Typography & Color

The typography is a strategic blend of a bold, handwritten-style script for 'Bean & Brew'—evoking a friendly, artisanal vibe—and a clean sans-serif for 'Caffe' to add a touch of modern professionalism. The deep, monochromatic brown palette grounds the identity in earthiness, symbolizing the richness of roasted coffee.
